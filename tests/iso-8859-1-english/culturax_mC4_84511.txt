MARKETING COMMUNICATION DESIGN
Our principals have combined experience in all aspects of marketing, communication and design. In addition, we draw from the skills and talents of a wide variety of contract writers, editors, proofreaders, designers, and photographers.
Principal - Design Communication
Mark Bond's 25-year career includes early stints as graphic designer for award-winning ad agency Anson-Stoner in Orlando, Florida, and as art director for Florida Hospital, also in Orlando. In 2000, he stepped out to begin his own design firm, which eventually transformed into Bond Communication & Design. His clients have included Disney, Universal Studios, and Carrier. He has also worked as a communication director and magazine editor.
Principal - Marketing Communication
Conna Bond completed her law degree at the University of Florida Levin College of Law, with initial aspirations to work in the field of business law. While interning with two corporate law firms in Orlando, she recognized her interest and strengths in communication and eventually developed a fascination with marketing communication and marketing strategy. She began collaborating with Mark on marketing and communication-related projects, which led to their current formal partnership as Bond Communication & Design. She has worked as a communication director and publications editor, and is currently an assistant professor teaching marketing, marketing management, consumer behavior, and business ethics at Walla Walla University in College Place, Washington. She holds memberships in the American Marketing Association and the Marketing Research Association.
